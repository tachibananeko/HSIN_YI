Origin Protection
  • Protection from Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack is a prominent feature of HSINY.
  • Requests from compromised machines from across the world could make your web service inaccessible.
  • The malicious machines use the public domain name service (DNS) looking for the IP address that hosts the domain. Then, it shuts it down by sending their traffic to that machine.
  • If an attacker tries to dismantle your site, HSINY network acts as a filter to block malicious traffic and only send legitimate traffic through to your origin server.
  • HSINY provides helpful tips to help keep your origin IP secure to protect.
